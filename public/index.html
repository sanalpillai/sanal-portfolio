About

I'm a growth and product analytics leader who's spent 6+ years at the intersection of data, product, and business strategy. I've worked at BCG building financial models for Fortune 500 clients, at Stripe sizing monetization experiments and diagnosing acquisition funnels, at Avanzar leading analytics across 60+ healthcare clients, and most recently at an AI healthtech startup measuring recommendation impact on user behavior.

I care about one thing: making sure the numbers actually change the decision. Not dashboards for dashboards' sake — but the kind of analysis that makes a PM reprioritize their roadmap or a VP reallocate budget mid-quarter.
