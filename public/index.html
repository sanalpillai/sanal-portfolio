About

The short version

I'm a growth and product analytics leader who's spent 6+ years at the intersection of data, product, and business strategy. I've worked at BCG building financial models for Fortune 500 clients, at Stripe sizing monetization experiments and diagnosing acquisition funnels, at Avanzar leading analytics across 60+ healthcare clients, and most recently at Mety measuring AI recommendation impact on user behavior.

I care about one thing: making sure the numbers actually change the decision. Not dashboards for dashboards' sake — but the kind of analysis that makes a PM reprioritize their roadmap or a VP reallocate budget mid-quarter.

M.S. Information Systems, Northeastern · B.E. Computer Science, Mumbai · I think in funnels, experiment in cohorts, and present in narratives.

Acquisition Funnel Diagnostics
Stripe · 380K+ sign-ups
Fintech▾

Problem

380K+ new users entering through four channels with wildly varying activation rates and no clear drop-off picture.

Approach

Segmented by entry point, mapped cohorts through onboarding, identified messaging mismatches between acquisition promise and activation UX.

Outcome

Lifecycle email entrants showed 2.3x higher activation with personalized onboarding. Findings directly shaped experiments.
