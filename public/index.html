About

The short version

I'm a growth and product analytics leader who's spent 6+ years at the intersection of data, product, and business strategy. I've worked at BCG building financial models for Fortune 500 clients, at Stripe sizing monetization experiments and diagnosing acquisition funnels, at Avanzar leading analytics across 60+ healthcare clients, and most recently at Mety measuring AI recommendation impact on user behavior.

I care about one thing: making sure the numbers actually change the decision. Not dashboards for dashboards' sake — but the kind of analysis that makes a PM reprioritize their roadmap or a VP reallocate budget mid-quarter.

M.S. in Information Systems from Northeastern · B.E. in Computer Science from University of Mumbai · I think in funnels, experiment in cohorts, and present in narratives.

Framework

How I approach a growth problem

My analytical process, from ambiguity to action.

01
Map the System
Understand the full user journey and business model before touching data. What are the levers? Where does value get created or lost?
02
Instrument & Measure
Define the right KPIs, ensure clean instrumentation, and establish baselines. Bad data in, bad decisions out.
03
Diagnose the Funnel
Find the structural drop-offs. Is it acquisition, activation, retention, or monetization? Each demands a different playbook.
04
Size & Prioritize
Not every insight deserves an experiment. Size the opportunity, estimate effort, and stack-rank ruthlessly.
05
Experiment & Measure
Design the test, define success criteria upfront, run it cleanly, and read it out honestly — including when it doesn't work.
06
Operationalize
If it works, scale it. Build the reporting, automate tracking, document the logic, and hand it off so it runs without you.

Acquisition Funnel Diagnostics
Stripe · 380K+ sign-ups analyzed
Fintech▾

The Problem

380K+ new users were entering through paid search, referrals, organic, and lifecycle email — but activation rates varied wildly, and nobody had a clear picture of where and why users were dropping off.

Approach

Segmented sign-ups by entry point and mapped each cohort through the onboarding flow. Identified messaging mismatches between acquisition promise and activation experience. Quantified drop-off at each step.

Outcome

Findings directly informed onboarding experiments. Surfaced that lifecycle email entrants had 2.3x higher activation when onboarding was personalized to their referral context.

AI Recommendation Impact Measurement
Mety Technology · 1.1M+ profiles analyzed
Healthtech▾

The Problem

The team needed to know if AI-generated lifestyle recommendations were driving sustained behavior change — or engagement that faded after first interaction.

Approach

Analyzed 1.1M+ health profiles to identify behavior segments. Linked engagement signals across 25K+ completed plans to sustained behavior change proxies. Built onboarding-to-engagement funnel models.

Outcome

Identified which recommendation types correlated with long-term retention vs. short-term spikes. Prioritized algorithm refinement and UX changes for highest-potential user segments.
